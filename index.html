What Is Byte Club?

Byte Club is a student run club aimed to teach computer programming, attend hackathons, and promote the hacking culture.

When is Byte Club Going to Launch?

Byte Club successfully launched on September 10th, you are welcome to join us on our following Byte Club Meetings.

Where Can I Sign Up?

Great, glad you're interested, stop by room 503 on the Main Campus every Thusday after school to sign up.

What Do I Need To Know?

Nothing! Our club is meant to accommodate coder of all levels. Experts or newbies.
